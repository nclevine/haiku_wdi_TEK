This is a Poem about a sunny spring day

This one sunny day
One of the first of spring
But it is still cold

About Haikus:
Haiku is one of the most important form of traditional Japanese poetry. Haiku is, today, a 17-syllable verse form consisting of three metrical units of 5, 7, and 5 syllables.
